About the VSEPR theory:

Contrary to popular belief, VSEPR does not stand for Vulcan-Starfleet Emergency Planned Response, but rather stands for Valence Shell Electron Repulsion. The theory assumes that each atom in a molecule will achieve a geometry that minimizes by arranging them so that they point in opposite directions, as far away as possible from each other.

About VSEPR shapes:

First things first: There is no relationship between a compound's formula and its VSEPR shape. The shapes can be predicted using Lewis Dot Diagrams and the VSEPR theory
