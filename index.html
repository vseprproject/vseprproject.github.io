Basic Overview:

About the VSEPR theory:

Contrary to popular belief, VSEPR does not stand for Vulcan-Starfleet Emergency Planned Response, but rather stands for Valence Shell Electron Repulsion. The VSEPR theory assumes that each atom in a molecule will achieve a geometry that minimizes the repulsion between electrons in the valence shell of that atom..

About VSEPR shapes:

As stated in the previous section, VSEPR shapes tend to place the electrons as far away as possible from each other. Given the large amount of combinations possible, there are many VSEPR shapes. VSEPR shapes include linear, trigonal planar, angled, tetrahedral, trigonal pyramidal, trigonal bipyramidal, disphenoidal (seesaw), t-shaped, octahedral, square pyramidal, square planar, and pentagonal bipyramidal.
